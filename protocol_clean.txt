Research Analysis Plan
Gastrointestinal Symptoms and Associated Factors Among University Students During Exam Periods
SPSS Only
Research Questions
What is the prevalence and severity of gastrointestinal symptoms among university students during exam periods?
What lifestyle and demographic factors are associated with GI symptom presence during exam periods?
What lifestyle and demographic factors are associated with total GI symptom severity score?
What factors predict self-reported worsening of GI symptoms during exam days compared to regular days?
Study Outcomes
Outcome
Definition
Measurement
Regression type

Dependent variable: GI_presence (0=No symptoms, 1=Any symptom present). SPSS: Analyze -> Regression -> Binary Logistic.
